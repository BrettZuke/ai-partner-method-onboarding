# Client Workspace — Notion Setup Guide

Build this workspace in Notion for every new creator client. Takes ~20 minutes.

**How to set it up:**
1. Create a new Notion page titled `[Client Name] — Workspace`
2. Build every section below in order
3. Share with client — give them **edit access** to their To-Do lists, **view access** to everything else
4. Populate the Brand Sheet from their Typeform answers once they've submitted

---

## Full Structure

---

### 📋 Onboarding To-Do List

A checklist the **client** works through. Link this at the top of every message you send them in week 1.

```
ONBOARDING CHECKLIST — complete these in order

[ ] Complete your intake form → [TYPEFORM LINK]
[ ] Share your software logins → [SECURE FORM LINK]
[ ] Book your kickoff call → [BOOKING LINK]
[ ] Review your Client Roadmap (in this workspace)
[ ] Film and send 5 content examples (raw is fine)
[ ] Confirm your IG access has been shared
```

### 🗓️ Content System (Kanban)

A Kanban board tracking every piece of content from idea to posted.

**Columns:**

| 💡 Idea | ✍️ Needs Script | ✅ Ready to Film | 🎬 Ready for Editing | 📅 Scheduled | ✔️ Posted |
|---------|----------------|-----------------|---------------------|-------------|----------|

**Each card includes:**
- Content idea / working title
- Platform(s) — IG / YT / TikTok
- Format — reel / carousel / talking head / etc.
- Hook
- Script (added when written)
- Raw footage link (added when filmed)
- Final edit link (added when edited)
- Post date (added when scheduled)
- Post link (added when live)

**Also attach:** a simple monthly calendar view showing what goes out on which day.

## Notes

- Keep the Brand Sheet updated as you learn more about their voice
- Review the Roadmap at the start of every month
- Add learnings to the SOP Database as you discover better ways of doing things
- The Content Kanban is the single source of truth for what content is in progress
